Ali Mozhdehi Fard - About

Hi, my name is Ali Mozhdehi Fard. I graduated from the Babol Noshirvani university of technology with a Master's in fluid and hydraulic Engineering in 2020 under the supervision of Dr. Rahmani Firoozjaee. After that, I began working as a Python developer and try to sharpen my skills in numerical modeling and machine learning.

Currently, I am working on computational fluid dynamics and Machine learning; also, I teach programming and English in my free time. My passion is to develop robust programs for numerical modeling of fluid behaviors. My research involves partial differential equations, computational fluid dynamics, and numerical methods in engineering. I develop applications using Python, FORTRAN, and MATLAB. Some of my codes are available on my GitHub. This website is to introduce my research fields and represent my skills, abilities, and experiences.

You may find further information about my education and work background in my CV.

  • Numerical Methods in Engineering
  • Machine Learning

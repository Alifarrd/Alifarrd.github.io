Climate Extremes, Energy Security & Geospatial Analysis

I investigate the intersections of climate change, public health, and energy demand in Central Asia. Using CMIP6 projections and ML-driven workflows, my research provides actionable insights for regional environmental resilience.

Current Research Trajectory

Temperature Extremes

Analyzing near-future and long-term (2022–2100) temperature shifts across 700+ locations in Central Asia using SSP2-4.5 and SSP5-8.5 scenarios.

Atmospheric Dust

Utilizing cluster-based frequency and intensity projections to study dust storm origins and atmospheric signals in the region.

Energy & Health

Quantifying the "Carbon Footprint" and shifting energy demands (Heating/Cooling Degree Days) resulting from heatwaves and climate shifts.
